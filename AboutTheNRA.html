What is the NRA?

The National Rifle Association is a gun rights advocacy group based in the United States. It was founded in 1871 as a recreational group designed to "promote and encourage rifle shooting on a scientific basis".

What have they contributed?

The association supported two major gun control acts, the National Firearms of 1934 (NFA) and Gun Control Act of 1968(GCA), but became politically actie following the passage of the GCA in the 1970s.
